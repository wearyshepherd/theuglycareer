About the Event

The Ugly Co. visited Career Day at Farmersville High School to showcase how we turn “ugly” fruit into delicious, sustainable snacks. Through live demos, free samples, and one-on-one chats, we connected with students ready to rethink food waste and explore careers in clean food innovation.

Event Highlights

🌱 Sustainability

We showed how upcycled fruit helps reduce waste and support local farms.

🍑 Product Tastings

Students sampled dried white peaches, cherries, and apricots straight from the source.

💼 Career Paths

From creative design to operations, we shared job roles that make food better.

📸 UGC Wall

We encouraged students to tag us using #UglyCareerDay for a chance to win some merch!
